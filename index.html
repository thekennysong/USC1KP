What is 1000 Pitches?

About the Competition

2 Month Long Pitch Competition

This competition is the platform for you to share your ideas with the world. We want to get as many pitches as possible to showcase the talent at USC –– so get going, you’ve got 2 months to submit up to 3 pitches!

10 Categories. $1000 Given to Each Category Winner.

We collect simple video recorded pitches in these 10 categories: consumer products and small business, environment, health, improve SC, education, tech and hardware, mobile apps, research, and pitch@oursponsors.

Schedule of the Competition

The structure of 1000 Pitches is simple. We begin with an opening ceremony to start the 2 month long sprint for pitches, have a marketing event midway through the process, and wrap up with an awards ceremony. Throughout the event, we will have pitch booths stationed on campus so you can pitch on the go and get your friends involved at their convenience.

The Judges and Judging Process

The judges are your fellow entrepreneurial trojans! We will be following a rubric set up by our friends at MPowered (hyperlink) and letting our sponsors help us decide the final winners.

1000 Pitches originated at the University of Michigan by their lead entrepreneurship council, MPowered. After much success, the competition then extended to UPenn. Now, USC is one of 9 universities nationwide participating in 1000 Pitches. Here at USC, we will be using MPowered platform for submitting pitches.
